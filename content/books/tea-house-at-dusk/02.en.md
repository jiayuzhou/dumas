# The Blue Envelope

@en-110 Without asking her name, the owner placed a blue envelope beside the cup.
@en-120 The paper was warm, as if it had just been folded.
@en-130 Inside, Mira found a letter addressed to **tomorrow**.
@en-140 She laughed once.
@en-150 Then she stopped, because the handwriting looked exactly like her own.
