Set in 1625, the novel follows the young Gascon d'Artagnan who leaves home for Paris, hoping to join the Musketeers of the Guard. Along the way he becomes entangled in a street brawl and inadvertently challenges three of the most formidable musketeers — Athos, Porthos, and Aramis — to duels. What begins as a confrontation quickly turns into an unbreakable friendship, and the four companions find themselves drawn into a web of court intrigue, political conspiracy, and personal honor involving Cardinal Richelieu, the Queen of France, and the mysterious Milady de Winter.

A classic tale of adventure, loyalty, and swashbuckling romance that has captivated readers since its serialization in 1844.
